Biggest Tech Firms


As of April 6, Apple’s sales top $200 billion, with a net profit of $45 billion (trailing just behind China’s ICBC) and assets of $260 billion. It’s also no secret that Apple is the world’s most valuable company with $740 billion in market cap, more than doubling that of Google, the world’s second most valuable company. Apple’s rival Samsung Electronics came second this year, edging out American giants Microsoft MSFT -7.22%, Google GOOGL -5.53%, and IBM IBM -0.54%. With $195 billion in sales, $22 billion in profits, and $210 billion in assets, Samsung holds a solid second place in those three metrics, with the exception of market cap where it in turns trails its American peers (Google, Microsoft, Facebook) and China’s e-commerce giant Alibaba .
